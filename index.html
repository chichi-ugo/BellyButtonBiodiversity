The human body is home to a host of different bacterial species! The diversity can be seen not only just from person to person, but even between different parts of the body. In an effort to uncover a bacterial species that synthesizes a protein that mimics the flavor of beef, the following data was collected from participants to culture and classify the bacterial species found growing in their navels!
